National Yoga Championship 2018
Have Represented our State in National Yoga Championship

With a focus on the importance of a robust body and an active lifestyle, the National Yoga Championship 2018, was organised by Rajas Vidhyalaya, ANDHERI (W) MUMBAI from 6th October, 2018 to 7th October, 2018 , for the children of 11-17 years of age. It was a great delight to see the participants perform various Asans with so much agility and ease. The team of DPS Agra had put their best foot forward to display their potential for yoga. Moreover, some kids have also got a golden opportunity to represent India in the International Yoga Championship which will be held in China ,in the month of December. The team made us proud with a stunning display of technical finesse and competitive spirit. The winners were felicitated by Ms. Pooja Bhatt and Mr. Deepak Tijori.

What's New

  • Provided with Air Conditioner in Each Classroom's

    Air conditioning is the perfect way to keep temperatures comfortable in hot weather. One of the places that will benefit most from air conditioning is a school. During the summer, classrooms can overheat very quickly on hot days which results in reduced learning. When students are exposed to the hot weather it can reduce concentration, problem solving, social skills and more. A comfortable classroom environment is essential for learning.

    Not only is a cool temperature important for learning but for health too. When anyone is exposed to hot temperatures for a long time it can lead to dehydration, heat stroke and more. Air conditioning is an essential for schools in hotter climates.

    Further to the recent communication from Dps Agra Administration regarding traffic and security, we would like to thank sincerely all parents and carers who routinely show their passes to the guards when they enter the school grounds.
